Hello there! Let me guide you to the better experience using our image viewer!

First, click on the button of the desired dataset to load it and visualize it!

You may have to wait a few seconds before being able to see the light field, but be pacient: I swear it'll worth it!

Then you can change the point of view moving along the point matrix. But remember: you need to choose the dataset first to be able to see the matrix!

Or you can use the slider to change the focus of the image selected.

The flower represents the nearest focal plane

And the mountains represents the farthest focal plane

Don't be shy, you can play as much as you want!

And if you have any doubts you can come back here anytime!
